Step 1: Become a junior generalist (2-3 years)

The first step in this path is to start getting experience so that you can join a high-upside early stage startup. To be appealing to one of these startups you need to:

  • Learn the basics of sales, marketing, operations, and how to communicate your ideas in the professional world
  • Identify a promising early stage startup
  • Learn the basics of strategy and get in tune with tech news
